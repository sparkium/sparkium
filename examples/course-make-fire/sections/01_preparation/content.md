# Preparation & Safety

## 1.1  Fire Triangle
Fire needs **heat**, **fuel**, and **oxygen**. Remove any one = no fire.

## 1.2  Safety Rules
- Clear 2 m radius down to mineral soil.
- Keep water & shovel within arm's reach.
- Never leave a fire unattended.

> 🔍 Download the [Safety Checklist](010-safety-checklist.pdf) and tick each box before you strike a spark.
