# Ignition Techniques

## 2.1  Ferrocerium Rod
Hold the rod at 45° and scrape **downwards** with the striker.

<video src="010-strike-demo.video.mp4" controls width="640"></video>

## 2.2  Bow Drill
1.  Cut a spindle & hearthboard of dry cedar.
2.  Loop the bowstring once around the spindle.
3.  Smoke = powder; keep going until an ember forms.
