# Extinguish & Leave-No-Trace

## 3.1  The Water-Stir-Water Rule
1.  Pour water on embers.
2.  Stir with a stick.
3.  Repeat until cold enough to touch.

## 3.2  Scatter Ashes
Once cold, scatter ashes over a wide area and restore the ground cover.
