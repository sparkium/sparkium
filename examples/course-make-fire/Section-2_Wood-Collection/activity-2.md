## Activity Overview

In this activity, you will practice identifying  
different types of firewood.

No fire will be lit.

---

## Instructions

### Step 1
Find or imagine three pieces of wood:
- one very small
- one medium-sized
- one thick piece

### Step 2
For each piece, answer:
- Is it dry or wet?
- Would it burn easily?
- Which category does it belong to?

---

## Reflection

- Which type was hardest to identify?
- What clues helped you decide?
