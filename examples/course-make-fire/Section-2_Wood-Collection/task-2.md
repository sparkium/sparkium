## Task Goal

Your goal is to collect and sort enough wood  
to prepare for making a fire.

No fire should be lit during this task.

---

## Requirements

Collect:
- a small bundle of tinder
- 10–15 pieces of kindling
- 2–3 pieces of fuel wood

---

## Completion

You are done when:
- all materials are dry
- each type is clearly separated
