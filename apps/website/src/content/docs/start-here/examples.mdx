If you are more of a "learning by doing" type, you might want to jump right into the examples. Also it's a bit tricky to show screenshots for this kind of tool, so maybe it's easier to understand what you can do with Sparkium by looking at some examples.

## Mastering the Swiss Flame

In the course "Mastering the Swiss Flame: Build Safe, Legal Bonfires" we will create a complete eLearning course about how to make a safe bonfire in Switzerland. We will cover all the steps from creating the content, to structuring it, to adding metadata, and finally to publishing it. This example will give you a good overview of what you can do with Sparkium and how it can help you to create your own eLearning materials. Have a look here: https://github.com/sparkium/sparkium/tree/main/examples/courses/bonfire.

Furthermore, it's always good to know, how to set up a safe bonfire in Switzerland. So even if you are not interested in eLearning, you might want to check it out. It's a win-win situation!
